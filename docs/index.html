"A naẓar (from Arabic ‏نَظَر‎ [ˈnaðˤar], meaning 'sight', 'surveillance', 'attention', and other related concepts) is an eye-shaped amulet believed to protect against the evil eye." - wikipedia.org

Focus Nazar is a simple, lightweight Chrome Extension designed to manage your focus.

You can choose you most "focus disrupting" services and limit their daily intake time or blocking them altogether.

Guides

Adding predefined domain
  1. Choose the "Predefined" tab
  2. Select your desired target
  3. Define daily limit or block altogether
  4. Add new entry!
Adding custom domain
  1. Choose the "Custom" tab
  2. Fill input with desired domain
  3. Define daily limit or block altogether
  4. Add new entry!
